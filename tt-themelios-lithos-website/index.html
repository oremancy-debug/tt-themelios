Who We Are

Excellence in Construction Since 2010

Founded in 2010, T.T. Themelios Lithos Ltd has become a trusted name in construction and renovation across Cyprus.

Led by Mr. Tryfonas Tryfonos, who brings over 25 years of international experience (including a decade in the U.S.), our company specializes in high-quality residential and commercial projects. From renovations and restorations to the construction of villas and apartment buildings, we are committed to craftsmanship, efficiency, and integrity in every project we undertake.
